Steps for Storing Vegetables Properly

  1. Wash and dry before storing.
  2. Use perforated bags or containers.
  3. Label by date.
  4. Keep leafy greens separate from ethylene producers.
  5. Store at proper temperature zones.

How to Choose Fresh Vegetables

  • Look for vibrant colors.
  • Avoid wilted leaves or bruises.
  • Check firmness and smell.
